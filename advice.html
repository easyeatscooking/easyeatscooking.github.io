Welcome to the advice page! We know people might struggle with cooking, but don’t be discouraged! Here are some tips to help you.

  • Always preheat the oven first so you aren't waiting for the oven to heat up.

  • Get out all the ingredients needed before you start cooking, so you know for sure you have all the ingredients.

  • Never use dull knives. It is safer to use sharpened knives.

  • Use a baking scale for the most accurate measurements.

  • Always clean your surface before and after cooking. DDon't forgrt to clean after each step that involves raw meet.

  • You should have at least two cutting boards (one for raw meat and one for everything else) to avoid contamination.

  • Don’t blindly follow the baking times for a recipe. Every oven is different. Older ovens still work, but the temperature may be different. For example, if a recipe states to put the food in for 15 minutes, you should put it in the oven for 10 minutes, and then check to prevent burning the food.

  • When cutting meat or vegetables aim to make the pieces similar sizes so they cook at the same time.
