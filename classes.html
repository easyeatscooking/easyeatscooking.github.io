Come join us for an online or in-person class! We offer a multitude of classes. We have beginner classes, advanced classes, seasonal classes, dessert classes, and even more! Our teachers are the best of the best, and will help you with anything you need during your session. Classes are typically 2-3 hours long.

Some Things to Know

  • You can sign up for a series of classes or a one time lesson.
  • Our teachers are experts with culinary degrees.
  • After your 5th session you can get the 6th for free.
  • We offer a student discount.

Payment

  • Our in person classes are $50 per person, or $85 for every two.
  • For classes with multiple sessions it is $40 per session.
  • Online classes are $30 per person
  • You must pay in-person with cash, card or online. We do not accept checks.
  • Payment must be made before your session.

Online Classes

  • We do not provide materials.
  • Before the class you will be sent an email of everything needed for the class.

In Person Classes

  • We provide everything needed for the class, you just need to show up.
  • We charge a $15 no show fee unless you give us notice.

Allergies

  • If you have any allergies please let us know!
  • We can substitute some ingredients; however, you need to let us know in advance.
  • Our kitchens are thoroughly cleaned, as well as our materials.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or allergies please contact us.
